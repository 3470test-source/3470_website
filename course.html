Courses Overview

Our medical coding training programs are designed to help students develop professional coding skills required in the healthcare industry. Explore our range of courses from beginner to advanced levels, including specialized certifications like CPC, CPMA, and CCS.

Medicals Coding

Learn the fundamentals of medical coding, including ICD (International Classification of Diseases), CPT (Current Procedural Terminology), and HCPCS (Healthcare Common Procedure Coding System) standards. Gain practical skills to accurately code medical records, ensuring compliance and proper billing in healthcare settings.

BMCT - Basic Medical Coding Training

Learn the fundamentals of medical coding, including anatomy, physiology, and coding guidelines for beginners. Understand ICD, CPT, and HCPCS coding standards and gain practical skills to accurately code medical records.

Duration: 40 hours

Days: 10 days

AMCT - Advanced Medical Coding Training

Take your medical coding skills to the next level with advanced coding techniques, compliance best practices, and auditing methods for healthcare professionals. Gain expertise in ICD, CPT, and HCPCS coding, and learn how to ensure accurate and efficient billing in complex healthcare settings.

Duration: 60 hours

Days: 15 days

CPC - Certified Professional Coder

Gain mastery in coding guidelines and prepare for the AAPC CPC certification. Learn in-depth CPT, ICD-10, and HCPCS coding standards while developing practical skills for accurate medical record coding and compliance.

Duration: 50 hours

Days: 12 days

EM - Evaluation & Management

Master E/M documentation and coding standards, including time-based coding, medical decision-making, and patient encounter workflows. Gain practical skills to accurately document and code evaluation and management services for compliance and efficient billing.

Duration: 30 hours

Days: 8 days

IPDRG - Inpatient Coding Diagnosis Related Groups

Learn the principles of DRG assignments and the application of ICD-10-CM and ICD-10-PCS coding in inpatient settings. Gain practical skills to accurately code hospital admissions, ensure proper reimbursement, and maintain compliance with healthcare regulations.

Duration: 35 hours

Days: 9 days

ED - Emergency Department

Learn emergency room documentation standards, including proper E/M level determination and accurate CPT code selection for ED visits. Develop practical skills to document patient encounters, code emergency services correctly, and ensure compliance with healthcare regulations.

Duration: 25 hours

Days: 7 days

CCS - Certified Coding Specialist

Prepare for the AHIMA CCS certification by mastering advanced inpatient and outpatient coding principles. Learn ICD-10-CM, ICD-10-PCS, CPT, and HCPCS coding standards, and develop practical skills to accurately code complex medical records for compliance and reimbursement.

Duration: 55 hours

Days: 14 days

CPMA - Certified Professional Medical Auditor

Develop expertise in auditing medical documentation, ensuring compliance, and verifying coding accuracy in healthcare organizations. Learn advanced auditing techniques, risk assessment, and best practices to maintain regulatory compliance and improve revenue integrity.

Duration: 45 hours

Days: 12 days

CRC - Certified Risk Adjustment Coder

Gain expertise in risk adjustment models and HCC (Hierarchical Condition Category) coding principles to ensure accurate reimbursement in value-based care. Learn advanced coding strategies, patient documentation review, and compliance techniques to maximize revenue and maintain healthcare quality standards.

Duration: 40 hours

Days: 10 days

IVR - Interactive Voice Response

Learn the fundamentals of IVR technology and how it integrates with healthcare communication systems. Understand call flow design, data handling, and automated patient interaction to improve efficiency in appointment scheduling, reporting, and patient data management.

Duration: 20 hours

Days: 5 days
