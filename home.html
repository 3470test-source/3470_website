Our Founder

Mr. Baskaran Kannan

Founder & CEO, 3470 Healthcare & Medical Coding Institutes

Mr. Baskaran Kannan is the visionary founder behind 3470 Healthcare. With a passion for excellence and a commitment to empowering healthcare professionals, he leads one of the premier medical coding training institutes dedicated to shaping the future of healthcare experts.

With over a decade of experience in the medical coding field, he holds prestigious credentials from AAPC as a Certified Professional Coder (CPC) and Certified Professional Medical Auditor (CPMA), as well as the Certified Coding Specialist (CCS) accreditation from AHIMA. These qualifications, combined with his dedication, inspired him to establish 3470 Healthcare in May 2016.

At 3470 Healthcare, we take pride in offering comprehensive medical coding training programs designed to meet the evolving demands of the healthcare industry.
