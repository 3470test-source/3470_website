IPDRG - Inpatient Coding Diagnosis Related Groups

The Inpatient Coding Diagnosis Related Groups (IPDRG) course trains students to code hospital inpatient cases accurately according to DRG systems. It covers diagnosis coding, procedure coding, and grouping methodologies to ensure accurate billing, compliance, and reimbursement for inpatient services.

Mode: Online & Offline

Duration: 60 Days

Batches: Weekdays & weekend
