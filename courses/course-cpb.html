Certified Professional Biller (CPB)

CPB - Overview

The Certified Professional Biller (CPB) certification focuses on medical billing processes, insurance claim submission, reimbursement methodologies, and revenue cycle management. This course prepares candidates to handle billing operations in physician offices, hospitals, and healthcare organizations.

What is CPB?

CPB (Certified Professional Biller) is a certification offered by AAPC. It validates expertise in medical billing, insurance guidelines, claims processing, and payer regulations.

CPB-certified professionals manage the financial side of healthcare, ensuring accurate claim submission and proper reimbursement.

Importance of CPB

  • High demand in billing & revenue cycle roles
  • Reduces claim denials and payment delays
  • Improves cash flow for healthcare providers
  • Strong career growth in medical billing
  • Recognized credential in healthcare finance

Responsibilities of a CPB Professional

  • Submit insurance claims accurately
  • Verify patient eligibility & benefits
  • Handle claim denials & appeals
  • Follow up on unpaid claims
  • Ensure compliance with payer policies
  • Support revenue cycle management processes

Eligibility

  • Basic knowledge of medical billing
  • Understanding of insurance processes
  • Experience in billing (Preferred)

Syllabus

  • Medical Billing Fundamentals
  • Insurance & Payer Guidelines
  • Claims Processing & Appeals
  • Revenue Cycle Management
  • Compliance & Regulations
  • Case Studies & Practical Training
