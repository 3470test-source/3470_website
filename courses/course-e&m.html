E&M - Evaluation & Management

The Evaluation & Management (E&M) course teaches students how to code, document, and manage patient encounters effectively. It covers office visits, consultations, and hospital-based encounters, following CPT and ICD-10 guidelines to ensure accurate billing and compliance with healthcare standards.
