Certified Evaluation & Management Coder (CEMC)

CEMC - Overview

The Certified Evaluation & Management Coder (CEMC) certification focuses on accurate coding of Evaluation and Management (E/M) services provided by physicians and healthcare professionals.

This course provides in-depth knowledge of E/M guidelines, documentation requirements, medical decision-making (MDM), time-based coding, and compliance standards under ICD-10-CM, CPT, and HCPCS coding systems.

Importance of CEMC

  • Ensures accurate physician E/M coding
  • Reduces claim denials and underpayments
  • Improves documentation compliance
  • Supports audit readiness
  • Enhances revenue cycle performance

Responsibilities of a CEMC Professional

  • Review physician documentation
  • Assign correct E/M levels
  • Apply Medical Decision Making (MDM) guidelines
  • Perform time-based coding when applicable
  • Ensure compliance with payer policies
  • Support audit and quality review processes

Career Opportunities

E/M Coding Specialist, Physician Coder, Medical Coding Auditor, Compliance Analyst, Revenue Cycle Executive.

Work Settings

Hospitals, Physician Clinics, Multi-specialty Centers, Medical Billing Companies, RCM Firms.

Certification Level

Specialized certification in Evaluation & Management (E/M) Coding.
